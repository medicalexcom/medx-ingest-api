# Channel: Amazon Listing

This channel is plain-text oriented and should avoid HTML-heavy layouts.

## Output Constraints
- No HTML tags in customer-facing listing fields.
- Title target: 150-200 characters (profile rules still apply).
- Feature bullets: up to 5 concise bullets.
- Description: 2-3 short paragraphs, factual and scannable.
- No internal links section.
- No manuals section.
- No FAQ section unless explicitly required by schema.

## Content Policy
- Keep claims grounded in packet evidence.
- Avoid prohibited/regulated claims unless explicitly sourced.
- Do not add marketplace policy-risk language.

## Formatting
- Start bullets with clear benefit or spec anchor.
- Avoid emoji and excessive punctuation for Amazon-style compliance.
