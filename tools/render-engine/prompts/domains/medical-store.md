# Domain: Medical Store

Use this domain profile for medical equipment/supply storefronts.

## Tone & Audience
- Professional, clinically aware, and caregiver-friendly.
- Prioritize clarity, safety, and practical usability.

## Regulatory/Claims Guardrails
- Do not imply diagnosis, treatment efficacy, or reimbursement eligibility unless explicitly sourced.
- Mention FDA/CE/ISO/HCPCS/Medicare only when present in trusted source inputs.
- Avoid medical overpromises.

## Content Priorities
- Grounded clinical relevance and product utility.
- Accurate specification reporting.
- Clear user suitability language when provided by source.
